Founder's Briefs

- Notes from Karl Sikkenga -

Prince Hal

‘Prince Hal’ is our longest script, but it comprises three of our shortest. Like our ‘Julius Caesar’, we distilled it way down to a single narrative—there’s really no B plot. This is the story of one young man. This single script links ‘Henry IV, Pt I’, ‘Henry IV, Part II’, and ‘Henry V’. One thing that we lost is the women saying, ‘My man! Please don’t go!’ Come on. Those women knew what they were getting into. It was a slew-or-be-slain kind of world.
