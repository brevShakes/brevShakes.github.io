Founder's Briefs

- Notes from Karl Sikkenga -

May 27: Merry Wives

Our ‘Merry Wives of Windsor’ was set in the 1977 Southern California of Fleetwood Mac, bell bottoms, and lava lamps. Of course we had beanbag chairs. Of course Falstaff was hidden underneath a pile of them at some point. Of course the merry wives beat the hell out of him with other beanbag chairs. And of course one of the beanbag chairs sprung a leak while in full swing. An arc of snowy styrofoam pellets soared through the air and ultimately carpeted the entire playing area for Acts III, IV, and V. One actor made an entrance, paused, stepped out again, and returned with a broom, which he used to sweep the stage during the next scene.
