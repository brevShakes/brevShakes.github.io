- Notes from Karl Sikkenga -

April 29: Seven Marriages

‘As You Like It’ in its proper form has twenty-five or thirty characters, and concludes (or, ahem, climaxes) in four marriages. We reduced the script to fourteen characters (plus the god of marriage); therefore it only made sense to finish with seven marriages. Taking this conceit a step further, we decided that, in a play about natural love, each of the seven matches was the right one for the fourteen partners. The arc for each character was the same: How did you manage to find the true love of your life?
